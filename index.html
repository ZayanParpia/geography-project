My Hajj Journey

Day 1 - Arrival at King Abdulaziz International Airport

We will arrive at King Abdulaziz International Airport in Jeddah. It will be a busy and modern place, close to many attractions, making it a great start to our journey.

Day 2 - Checking-in at Movenpick Hotel

We will then check into the Movenpick Hotel in Mecca, one of the best 5-star hotels in the area.

Day 3 - Dining at Al Baik

We will visit Al Baik, a famous fried chicken restaurant in Mecca. Known for its tasty fast food, it will be an experience to remember.
